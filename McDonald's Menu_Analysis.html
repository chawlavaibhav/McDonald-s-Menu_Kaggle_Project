Introduction¶

The dataset provides a nutrition analysis of every menu item on the US McDonald's menu, including breakfast, beef burgers, chicken and fish sandwiches, fries, salads, soda, coffee and tea, milkshakes, and desserts. There are 260 rows and corresponding to them, information about 24 nutritional parameters. This notebook is aimed to study the interesting facts about nutritional value of McDonald's menu. I t exploresthe relationship between various parameters.

#Function to plot bar graphs
def barplotter(grouped):
    item = grouped["Item"].sum()
    item_list = item.sort_index()
    item_list = item_list[-20:]
    #Sizing the image canvas
    plt.figure(figsize=(8,9))
    #To plot bargraph
    g = sns.barplot(item_list.index,item_list.values)
    labels = [aj.get_text()[-50:] for aj in g.get_yticklabels()]
    g.set_yticklabels(labels)
